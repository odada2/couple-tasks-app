## Introduction

Welcome to Couple Tasks ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Couple Tasks, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Name** (from Google Sign-In)
- **Email address** (from Google Sign-In)
- **Profile photo** (optional, from Google Sign-In)
- **User ID** (generated automatically)

### 2. Task and Collaboration Data

When you use the app, we collect:
- **Tasks** you create (title, description, due date, completion status)
- **Nudges** you send to your partner
- **Couple relationship** data (partner linking information)
- **Invite codes** you generate

### 3. Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **Device information** (device type, operating system)
- **App usage analytics** (features used, session duration)
- **Crash reports** (for debugging and improving the app)
- **Performance metrics** (app load times, response times)

### 4. AI Interaction Data

When you use AI features:
- **Messages** you send to the AI assistant
- **AI-generated suggestions** and responses
- **Task suggestions** you request

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### 1. Provide and Maintain the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Enable task collaboration with your partner
- Sync data across your devices
- Provide AI-powered features and suggestions

### 2. Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

### 3. Communicate with You
- Send important service updates
- Respond to your support requests
- Send push notifications (if enabled)

### 4. Ensure Security
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Monitor for suspicious activity
- Protect against unauthorized access

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Your Data With:

#### 1. Your Partner
- When you link with a partner, they can see:
  - Shared tasks you create
  - Nudges you send
  - Your basic profile information (name, photo)

#### 2. Service Providers
- **Google Firebase**: Authentication, database, analytics, and crash reporting
- **Google Gemini AI**: AI-powered task suggestions and chat features
- **Google Cloud Platform**: Data storage and processing

#### 3. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

### We Do NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Use your data for marketing purposes without consent
- ❌ Share your tasks or personal information publicly

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Firebase Security Rules**: Strict access controls on database
- **Authentication**: Secure Google Sign-In
- **Regular audits**: Ongoing security reviews

### Data Location

Your data is stored on:
- **Google Firebase** servers (Cloud Firestore)
- **Google Cloud Storage** (for media files)
- **Local device storage** (for offline functionality)

Data is stored in accordance with Google's data center locations and security standards.

## Data Retention

### We Retain Your Data:

- **Active accounts**: As long as your account is active
- **Deleted tasks**: Permanently deleted immediately
- **Closed accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Analytics data**: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for 26 months
- **Crash reports**: Retained for 90 days

### To Delete Your Account:

1. Contact us at [support email]
2. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days
3. Your partner will be notified of the unlinking
